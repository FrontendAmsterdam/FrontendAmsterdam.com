React Day Berlin

React Day Berlin is a brand new event in the city, the first of its kind — a full day conference for engineers working with the React technology stack.

Careful balance of practical and visionary talks from renowned speakers make React Day a unique place to meet and learn from the most forward-thinking international community members, influencers and open source maintainers.

https://reactday.berlin

  • Want to sponsor?

Each of our conferences originates from a local community and Berlin is not an exception. In collaboration with number of local activists, we're organizing around 6-8 meetups a year, giving space for networking and new public speakers growth.

https://www.meetup.com/React-Berlin-Meetup

React Advanced is a place for engineers to gather and share insights on their biggest challenges they have faces through building large-scale, complex applications. Through a combination of expert level talks, advice lounge, and numerous hads-on workshops - we help these insights to be shared with local and global communities in the most open and accessible way.

https://reactadvanced.com

  • Want to sponsor?

Join our quarterly meetup events in London, to get a feel of what our community is challenged with these days and submit your proposals to talk about own learnings!

https://www.meetup.com/React-Advanced

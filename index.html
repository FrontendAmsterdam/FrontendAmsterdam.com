React
Amsterdam

React Amsterdam is a meetup group and a full day conference on all things React. Being the oldest ReactJS community in BeNeLux it gather Front End developers across the globe in the tech heart of Europe. With internationally recognized speakers, amazing attendee crowd and a top location, sponsoring our event is a brilliant opportunity for you to put your company name into spotlight, rally up your team or hire the best.

AmsterdamJS

The largest and longest-running JavaScript meetup group in town (and whole NL)! AmsterdamJS unites experienced JavaScript developers and newcomers just got excited by the Web Platform.

Apart from being the awesome local meetup, on June 2017 the first, full day AmsterdamJS conference will take place at an incredible location in the center of Amsterdam.

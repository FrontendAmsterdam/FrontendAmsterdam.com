    Who we are?

    We see technical excellence and engineering culture as a major values and a significant part of our mission which you are welcome to join.

    GitNation is a foundation with a goal to contribute to the development of the open source landscape by organizing events around open source technology that are meaningful, actual, and fun.

    Events connecting talented engineers, researchers and OSS authors.
    We support open source projects by financing (apply for a grant) and we celebrate OSS success at the annual Open Source Awards ceremony.

    We are a foundation which celebrates diversity.
    This is why for every conference we provide diversity scholarships for attending and run mentorships programs to help underrepresented in tech with getting to the stage as speakers.

    GitNation was founded by engineers and community activists.
